How to play
🔀 Swipe or use arrow keys to slide all tiles.
➕ Two matching numbers collide? They merge and double.
🔤 Hit 128 and that tile becomes a mystery letter — revealed in its correct position.
🎯 Feeling confident? Type your guess and hit Guess — but you only get one shot per round!
🔁 Complete each word and the board carries on with a longer word next round.
↩️ Beat the 9-letter word and the words get shorter again — 9→8→7→6→5→4. Can you go the full distance?
